Some of the information we collect:

When you create or reconfigure an account with Dualities, you provide some personal information, including, but not limited to name and email address.  Further, residential and/or business information including address, state, zip code, etc. may be collected. Some of this information, for example, your name and username, may be listed on our Services, including on your profile page and in search results.   • Account Information. This includes at least your user name, and e-mail address;

  • Network Performance & Usage Information. This tells us how you use our network, our products and our services, and how well our equipment and network is performing;

  • Geolocation. We may collect your location through GPS, Wi-Fi, or wireless network triangulation in order to obtain your location for the purposes of providing our Service and further secure your account. We may use geolocation data to build a history of occurrences of observed events that are the intent of Dualities, in order to bring the most relevant information to you.

  • Cookies. Like many websites, we use cookies and similar technologies to collect additional website usage data and to improve our Services, but we do not require cookies for many parts of our Services such as searching for user profiles. A cookie is a small data file that is transferred to your computer's hard disk. Dualities may use both session cookies and persistent cookies to better understand how you interact with our Services, to monitor aggregate usage by our users and web traffic routing on our Services, and to customize and improve our Services. Most Internet browsers automatically accept cookies. You can instruct your browser, by changing its settings, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit. However, some Services may not function properly if you disable cookies.

  • Log Data. Our servers may automatically record information ("Log Data") created by your use of the Services. Log Data may include information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, the referring web page, pages visited, location, your mobile carrier, device and application IDs, search terms, and cookie information. We may receive Log Data when you interact with our Services, for example, when you visit our website(s) or use our apps or otherwise sign into our Services, interact with our email notifications, use your Dualities account to authenticate to a third-party website or application, or visit a third-party website that includes a Dualities button or widget. Dualities uses Log Data to provide our Services and to measure, customize, and improve them. If not already done earlier, we will either delete Log Data or remove any common account identifiers, such as your username, full IP address, or email address, after 18 months.

  • Third-Parties. Dualities may use a variety of third-party services to help provide our Services, such as hosting our website(s), and to help us understanding the use of our Services, such as Google Analytics. These third-party service providers may collect information sent by your browser as part of a web page request, such as cookies or your IP address.

Details on Personal and Anonymous & Aggregate Information

  • What is Personal Identifiable Information? It is information which, when used alone or with other relevant data, can identify or can reasonably be used to identify an individual Customer or User.

  • What is Anonymous Information? This is information which cannot be used to identify and cannot reasonably be used to identify an individual Customer or User.

  • What is Aggregate Information? It is information about groups or categories of Customers or Users, which does not identify and cannot reasonably be used to identify an individual Customer or User. Aggregate Information is a kind of Anonymous Information.

  • How do we use this information? We use this information for a number of activities comprising research, data analysis and marketing.

Our privacy Commitments

  • We do not sell your Personal Information to anyone for any purpose.

  • We keep your Personal Information in our records for as long as you use our Services, or until it is no longer needed for business, tax, or legal purposes.

  • We keep your information secure by using encryption, state-of-the-art security controls and by following industry security best practices.

Just deleting our application from your mobile device will NOT delete your account. By deleting your account you acknowledge that you will lose access to all of your documents, messages, photos and data secured by our Services, with no way to recover them.
