If you have any questions or comments about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@cyphlens.com.

  1. Information We Collect

    1. Information Collected Upon Registration. When you create or reconfigure an account with Cyphlens, you provide some personal information, including, but not limited to name and email address. Additional residential and/or business information such as address, state and zip code may also be collected. Some of this information, for example, your name and username, may be listed on our Services, including on your profile page and in search results. Some Services, such as search and viewing public user profiles, may not require registration.

    4. Geolocation. We may collect your location through GPS, Wi-Fi, or wireless network triangulation to provide our Service and further secure your account. We may use geolocation data to build a history of occurrences of observed events to bring the most relevant information to you.

    5. Cookies. Like many websites, we use cookies and similar technologies to collect additional website usage data and to improve our Services, but we do not require cookies for many parts of our Services such as searching for user profiles. A cookie is a small data file that is transferred to your computer's hard disk. Cyphlens may use both session cookies and persistent cookies to better understand how you interact with our Services, to monitor aggregate usage by our users and web traffic routing on our Services, and to customize and improve our Services. Most Internet browsers automatically accept cookies. You can instruct your browser, by changing its settings, to stop accepting cookies or to prompt you before accepting a cookie from the websites you visit. However, some Services may not function properly if you disable cookies.

    6. Log Data. Our servers may automatically record information ("Log Data") created by your use of the Services. Log Data may include information such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, the referring web page, pages visited, location, your mobile carrier, device and application IDs, search terms, and cookie information. We may receive Log Data when you interact with our Services, for example, when you visit our website(s), use our apps, sign into our Services, interact with our email notifications, use your Cyphlens account to authenticate to a third-party website or application, or visit a third-party website that includes a Cyphlens button or widget. Cyphlens uses Log Data to provide our Services and to measure, customize, and improve them. If not already done earlier, we will either delete Log Data or remove any common account identifiers, such as your username, full IP address, or email address, after 18 months.

    7. Third-Parties. Cyphlens may use a variety of third-party services to help provide our Services, such as hosting our website(s), and to help us understanding the use of our Services, such as Google Analytics. These third-party service providers may collect information sent by your browser as part of a web page request, such as cookies or your IP address.
